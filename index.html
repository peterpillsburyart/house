The neighborhood

Minutes from Top of the World

The house sits just below Alta Laguna Park — the hilltop locals call "Top of the World," one of the highest points in Laguna Beach. It's a short walk up, and the views stretch from the open ocean and Catalina all the way back across the canyons.

From the park's edge you can step straight onto the trails of Aliso and Wood Canyons Wilderness Park, then come home for a swim. A rare mix of coastline and quiet hillside, all within reach.

  • Panoramic ocean & canyon views
  • Miles of walking and hiking trails
  • Tennis and pickleball courts
  • Picnic tables and grassy lawns
  • Open late for sunset
  • The beach, about a mile away
